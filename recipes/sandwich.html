Veg Club Sandwich

Description

Veg Club Sandwich is a traditional sandwich with veggies, mayonnaise filling , diced tomatoes layered in brown bread slices , grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 1 medium cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 small capsicum, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • 4-5 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for toasting

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown. Spread butter on one side of each slice.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on three slices and mashed avocado on the other three.
  3. On the slices with mayonnaise, layer lettuce, cucumber, tomato, onion, and capsicum.
  4. Season with salt and pepper as per taste.
  5. Top the vegetable-laden slices with the avocado-spread slices to form sandwiches.
  6. Cut each sandwich diagonally and serve immediately.
