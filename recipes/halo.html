Halo Halo

Description

Halo-Halo is a popular Filipino dessert that consists of a mix of various ingredients such as sweetened fruits, jellies, and beans, topped with crushed ice, evaporated milk, and sometimes a scoop of ice cream.
The name "Halo-Halo" literally means "mix-mix" in Filipino, reflecting the way the dessert is prepared and eaten.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened white beans
  • 1/4 cup sweetened banana (saba) or plantains, sliced
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco (coconut jelly)
  • 1/4 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shaved or crushed ice
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 scoop of ube (purple yam) ice cream
  • Leche flan (optional, as topping)

Steps

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened red beans, white beans, banana, nata de coco, kaong, and sweetened jackfruit.
  2. Add the shaved or crushed ice on top of the layered ingredients.
  3. Pour the evaporated milk over the ice, allowing it to seep through to the bottom layers.
  4. Place a scoop of ube ice cream on top of the ice.
  5. Optionally, add a slice of leche flan as an additional topping.
  6. Serve immediately and mix the ingredients thoroughly before eating.
